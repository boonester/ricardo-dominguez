Flood Net was a tool programmed by EDT as a way of staging “virtual sit-ins” in solidarity with the Zapatistas. These virtual sit-ins consist of users using the tool to stage DoS and DDoS attacks against the websites of Mexican president Ernesto Zedillo and the Pentagon. These attacks would overload the site with politicized error logs, leading to users of the websites seeing messages along the lines of “human_rights not found on this server.”
